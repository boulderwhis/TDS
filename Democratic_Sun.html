Welcome!

In Development, an app that aims to spread microdonations accross legislators dependent upon issue engagement. Currently building out the db.

Over the past decade the ability of the People to influence public policy has been greatly diminished due to the increased presence and legal abilities of special interest groups. This monetary influence has lead to the over represenation of corporate interests and under represenation of the collective will. This tool aims to correct that imbalance by providing, for the user, an issue based data-driven solution. When you choose an issue to become an engaged in your small donation will be automatically added to a pool of other donations and diseminated out to influence those individuals in Congress that have a proven track record of fighting for it; or it may be used in an effort to decrease the volume of those that fight against it by contributing directly to the campaign chests of those running against them.
